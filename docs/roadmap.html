Phase 2 · MVP
Analysis & Target Ranges
~3–4 weeks
▼
The heart of the app. This is where the real value over the FS Golf App comes in — your own target ranges, your own feedback system.
🎯
Target Ranges (Margins)
Set min/max per club and metric. Visual feedback: green/red. Face-to-path 2°–4° is the classic use case.
per clubdraw-bias
💥
Face Impact View
Club face as graphic, impact point as dot. Heatmap across multiple shots. Requires FIL add-on.
FIL add-onSVG
📈
Session Statistics
Averages, standard deviation, target hit rate, draw percentage. Per session and per club.
useShotStats
📍
Shot Dispersion
Landing points as scatter plot on a fairway/green graphic. Very intuitive at a glance.
SVGHLA + carry
📅
Session History
All sessions in chronological order. Per session: date, club, shot count, avg carry. Detail view per session.
history
📉
Trend Charts
Metric development over time (e.g. AoA across last 10 sessions). Shows whether swing work is paying off.
victory-nativealready installed
Outcome: The app is now more useful than the FS Golf App for targeted practice — your own targets, your own analysis, no cloud required.
Phase 3 · v2
Video Integration
~4–6 weeks
▼
Complex, but high value — especially for EE diagnosis. Requires careful architecture for circular buffer and sync logic.
📷
Video Recording + Sync
Continuous recording, shot event as trigger, clip cut from buffer (2s pre / 3s post impact). Post-trigger first.
vision-cameracircular buffer
🎬
Video Player with Data
Play clip alongside shot metrics. Slow motion. Frame-accurate scrubbing.
expo-videoalready installed
✏️
Video Annotations
Draw lines and points: swing path, head position, hip line. Freeze a frame, then annotate.
Skia / SVG overlayreact-native-skia
📁
Video Storage
Save clips locally (expo-file-system), optionally upload to Supabase Storage. DB link to shot.
expo-file-systemSupabase Storage
🔀
Video Comparison
Play two shots side by side. Ideal for making swing changes visible over time.
v2 detail
Note: Implement post-trigger first (after impact only) — doable in 1–2 days. Circular buffer (pre-impact) is ~1 extra week, but worth it for DTL analysis.
